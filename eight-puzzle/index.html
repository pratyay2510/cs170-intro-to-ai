8-puzzle — BFS, DFS, UCS & play

Tiles 1–8 and one blank on a 3×3 grid. Goal: blank top-left, then 1–2 on the first row, 3–5, then 6–8. In play mode, slide tiles into the blank (or use arrow keys) within a move budget.

Formulation: A state is the tile placement; actions move the blank up, down, left, or right; each move has cost 1 (UCS matches BFS for fewest moves). Random states are generated by a random walk from the goal so they are always solvable.
